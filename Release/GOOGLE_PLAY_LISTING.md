# Google Play Listing

## App name

Lighthouses: Mystery of the Archipelago

## Short description

Restore a magical archipelago in a relaxing match-3 adventure.

## Full description

A storm has extinguished the lights of a distant archipelago. Match shells, coral, crystals and sunstones to restore its islands, rebuild the lighthouse and uncover the story hidden in the tide.

FEATURES

- Match three or more pieces across a colorful 8x8 board.
- Create beams, bombs and pearls for satisfying chain reactions.
- Clear ice, crates and seaweed through 20 levels.
- Earn stars, rebuild the lighthouse and unlock the next islands.
- Collect a daily reward and play entirely offline.
- No ads, no account and no forced connection in version 1.0.
